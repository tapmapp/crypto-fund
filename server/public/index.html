A little bit about me

Hello I'm Juan,

In 2016 when I learned about Bitcoin and the fundamental problem it was trying to solve regarding central banking monetary policy and inflation, I dove head first into the subject. I poured thousands of hours into learning about blockchain, bitcoin, ethereum, smart contracts, DeFi and the overall market trends. Besides the financial gains, I am excited about the impact it will have on humanity when there is mass adoption. Protecting privacy, meritocracy and accountability in society is important for me, and I want to be an active piece of this movement.

At this point of my career, after having worked mostly in Global Finance roles for the past 6 years, I decided to pivot fully into cryptocurrencies and have set up this consultancy to help onboard individuals and SME's. I hope to meet you soon to show you how passionate I am about my clients education and success within this space.

  • +7 years working in Corporate Finance

  • +5 years involved in Cryptocurrencies/Blockchain
